[Verse 1]

Dil mera ruk jave vekh standing so still
Kach de glass'an vich disda amber glow
Naina di chal teri moving so slow
Jadon turrdi ae tu hundi stopping the time
Tenu main takda haan lagda it's a crime
Kalla main sochan tenu out of my head
